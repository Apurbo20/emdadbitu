Emdadul Islam Bitu: Development Communication Visualizer.

Emdadul Islam Bitu is a development communication visualizer, photographer, photo journalist, consultant, researcher and founder of Darkroom photo agency established in 2010. Emdad and his Dark Room Photo agency have worked with several international development organizations in the country such as British Council, UNRCO, UNMC, UNDP, UNAIDS, UNICEF, FAO, IRG, Action Aid, Plan Bangladesh, Asia foundation, ICDDRB, DFID, GIZ, Qatar Red Crescent, Qatar Charity, Bloomberg Philanthropies, USP, Oxfam, Save the Children, Swisscontract, Concern Worldwide, Concern Universal, Resolve to save lives, ICCO, CIPRB as a documentation specialist/consultant/researcher. Emdadul Islam Bitu has huge number of lifestyle, agricultural, climate change and food security related documentation experiences As a result Emdad has published several photographic documentaries like, ‘Promising lives” Story of the Sponsored Children of Action Aid Bangladesh (published in 2010) ``Deb Barma” Tripura Culture near Sylhet (published in 2008) improving the living conditions of children of sex workers by Action Aid Bangladesh (published in 2013) “THROUGH THE LENS OF CDMP” A photo story by CDMP-UNDP, Published in Nov 2015. “United Nations Development Assistance. Framework”, UNDAF 2017-2020 Bangladesh. “EMPOWERD LIVES, RESILIENT BANGLADESH” a photographic journey, United Nations Development Program 2018. He had been working for 10 years as a photographer with Bangladeshi leading photo agency MAP and documented a number of social issues of Bangladesh as a documentary photographer. His interest and experience working in lifestyle documentation of various communities’ social problem, urban development, human rights, climate change, wild life. He had been working last 20 years with the wilderness and forested area in the lawachara National Forest, Chittagong Hill Tracts, Sundarban etc. Emdadul Islam Bitu is a passionate, enthusiastic, gentle and caring naturalist with strong determination towards his purpose.
